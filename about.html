ABOUT ME

I am Menna, a 19-year-old Palestinian from Gaza. As a computer engineering student, I was expected to complete my second year by now. I have intermediate experience in web programming and several programming languages, including Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and C++. I joined the Girls Geeks Camp, where I expanded my knowledge in various programming languages, business management, and UX/UI design. This camp ignited my passion for programming and web development. My curiosity about computers and programming led me to pursue a deep journey into technology. I believe it is our duty to contribute to making our future better, as it is not only ours but belongs to everyone. I chose to study computer engineering, but unfortunately, the ongoing genocide in my home our life has been paused to an unknown time . My university was bombed, and we have lost much. We have lost much, but I still have many dreams to achieve.
